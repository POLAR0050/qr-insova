Neuro Glove - Smart Rehabilitation System

Problem

Millions suffer from Parkinson’s and stroke. Current rehabilitation devices are expensive, bulky, and limited to clinics.

Solution

Neuro Glove is a wearable device that reads muscle signals and helps restore hand movement in real time using AI.

How It Works

EMG sensors detect muscle activity → AI processes data → vibration assists movement. Includes Automatic, Manual, and Therapy modes.

Devices

Includes smart spoon for independent eating and full control system.
